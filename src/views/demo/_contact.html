Contact Form

    No PHP, JS required. You only need to replace [YOUR_EMAIL_ADDRESS] with your email address. For more details, please checkout FORMSPREE.
    You can use disposable email provided by Mailinator for testing the contact form.
    <form action="https://formspree.io/[YOUR_EMAIL_ADDRESS]" method="POST" class="contact">
      <h4>Contact Us</h4>
      <input type="text" name="name" class="filled" placeholder="Your Name">
      <input type="email" name="_replyto" class="filled" placeholder="Your Email">
      <input type="submit" value="Send" class="btn-sm">
    </form>
